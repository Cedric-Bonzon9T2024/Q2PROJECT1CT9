Famous Filipino Foods

Adobo

Adobo is a Filipino stew made with pork or chicken, marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices.

Sinigang

Sinigang is a sour soup made with tamarind and various vegetables, often with pork, shrimp, or fish.

Lechon

Lechon is a whole roasted pig, crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, a Filipino favorite during celebrations.
